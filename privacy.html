Your privacy matters. Contacts.com is built to protect your data, not exploit it.

Privacy Policy

Contacts.com is designed with privacy at its core. We collect only the information needed to provide a fast, secure, and reliable experience. We never sell your data, and we never use it for targeted advertising.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, and profile details you choose to share.
  • Messages: Encrypted end‑to‑end. We cannot read your messages.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics to improve performance and reliability.
  • Device Data: Browser type, device type, and general technical information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your account and deliver core features.
  • To improve speed, reliability, and user experience.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access.

What We Never Do

  • We never sell your personal data.
  • We never show ads or track you across the web.
  • We never read your private messages.

Data Security

Messages are encrypted using industry‑standard encryption. Sensitive data is stored securely and protected from unauthorized access. We continuously improve our security practices to keep your information safe.

Your Rights

  • Update or delete your profile information.
  • Request account deletion.
